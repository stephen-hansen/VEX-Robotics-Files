Song conversion format
PlayTone(frequency, duration1); wait1Msec(duration2); per note

duration 2 = duration 1 * about 10.5
frequency is note based

assuming fifth octave;
523 = C
587 = D
659 = E
698 = F
783 = G
880 = A
987 = B

Add or decrease 350-450 to change octave respectively
